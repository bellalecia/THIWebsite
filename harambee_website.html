Our Mission

The Harambee Initiative is a place where holistic support meets tangible action in a unified community. We provide programs for youth and adult enrichment, senior services, education, economic development, and food insecurity.

Community Impact

Serving DuPage County with comprehensive programs addressing food insecurity, childcare, education, and senior services.

Holistic Approach

Addressing physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being in one unified space.

Together We Rise

Building the "Beloved Community" that Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. envisioned through collaborative action.

About The Harambee Initiative

Harambee, a Swahili word meaning "all pull together," represents our commitment to community unity and collective action. Founded on the critical work led by Reverend Conitras M. Houston Dickens, we are creating a transformative community hub in DuPage County.

Our Programs

Food Security Programs

Community garden and food pantry partnerships serving 50,000+ hungry families in DuPage County.

Early Childhood Education

Quality preschool programs for DuPage County's 46,000 children under five who lack access to childcare.

Senior Enrichment

Social connections and support services for isolated elderly community members.

STEAM Education

Digital skills training for 33% of workforce lacking tech skills for 92% of available jobs.

Youth Development

Storefront opportunities for local businesses to showcase products and stimulate economic growth.
