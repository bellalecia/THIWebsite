Our Mission

The Harambee Initiative is a place where holistic support meets tangible action in a unified community. We provide programs for youth and adult enrichment, senior services, education, economic development, and food insecurity.

Community Impact

Serving DuPage County with comprehensive programs addressing food insecurity, childcare, education, and senior services.

Holistic Approach

Addressing physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being in one unified space.

Together We Rise

Building the "Beloved Community" that Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. envisioned through collaborative action.
