About the Program

As a Grow with Google Partner, The Harambee Initiative is proud to offer the Google Career Certificates and Google Career Essentials Scholarship Program. This initiative provides access to industry-recognized certifications that can help launch your career in high-demand fields at no cost.

Available Certificates

Cybersecurity

Prepare for an entry-level role in cybersecurity. Learn how to identify common risks, threats, and vulnerabilities, as well as techniques to protect organizations.

Project Management

Master the basics of project management, including agile methodologies, leadership skills, and strategic planning. Learn to coordinate teams and deliver successful projects.

Data Analytics

Learn to collect, transform and analyze data using tools like SQL and Tableau. Make data-driven decisions and create compelling visualizations to tell data stories.

Digital Marketing & E-commerce

Develop skills in social media marketing, SEO, e-commerce strategies, and digital advertising. Learn to create and execute effective digital marketing campaigns.

UX Design

Learn user experience design fundamentals, including wireframing, prototyping, and user research. Create intuitive, accessible, and user-centered digital experiences.

IT Support

Master the fundamentals of IT support, including computer networking, system administration, security, and customer service. Prepare for an entry-level IT role.
